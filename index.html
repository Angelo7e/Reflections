R E F L E C T I O N S

Elijah Davis-Galentine

 In the historic French countryside of the mid-15th Century, the quaint and pious family of Iehan, Julienne, and little Nicolas have lived happily for many years, tending to their crop in relative peace; however, recent changes in the local weather have put hard strains on agrarian life. Many have been able to struggle through, but for this family, the storm has only just begun.
