R E F L E C T I O N S

Elijah Davis-Galentine

Did you enjoy your experience? I hope that you did. You wanted to know their story, and you followed their misery around every turn. You had the power to leave them, struggling, but happy, and it was your hand that sent them down this path; after all, in fiction, the characters can only experience the journey your mind lays out for them. They are as ephemeral as your reading, as extant as your emotion itself.

Was it worth it?



I want to go through again.
